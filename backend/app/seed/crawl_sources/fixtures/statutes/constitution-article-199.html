Constitution of Pakistan - Article 199

Constitution Article 199
Constitution of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, 1973
Article 199
Constitutional Remedy
English
Statute
Article 199 empowers a High Court to make an order on the application of an aggrieved party where a person performing functions in connection with the affairs of the Federation, a Province, or a local authority acts without lawful authority or in violation of law. The jurisdiction is equitable and discretionary, and the chamber must still address alternate remedy, urgency, and the exact public-law hook visible from the record.
