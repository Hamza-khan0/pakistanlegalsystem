Rules
Order VII Rule 11 deals with rejection of plaint where the plaint does not disclose a cause of action, where the relief claimed is undervalued and not corrected, or where the suit appears from the statements in the plaint to be barred by law. In chamber workflows it often operates as a threshold procedural hook rather than a final answer on contested facts.
