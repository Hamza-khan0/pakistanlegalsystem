Qanun-e-Shahadat Article 117
Qanun-e-Shahadat Order, 1984
Article 117
Burden of Proof
English
Statute
The burden of proof lies on the person who would fail if no evidence at all were given on either side. In civil and constitutional chamber work this provision often frames how aggressively counsel can assert a fact without annexure support.
